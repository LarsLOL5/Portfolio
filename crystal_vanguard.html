Crystal Vanguard

In this game, your goal is to survive the enemies and prevent them from reaching the gem. You do that by spawning friendly units. They will fight each other, so it's AI VS AI. There is a lot of strategy involved. The first reason is that there is an economic system. You will get money automatically and you can spend that on your units. The second reason is that some units are better against certain enemies than others. So choose wisely which units you are going to spawn. There are going to be levels at which the difficulty will scale up.
