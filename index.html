About me



Hi, my name is Lars Pluijmakers. I am currently 18 years old, living in Oudkarspel, and my hobbys are gaming (obviously), playing chess and ice-skateing. My education is Game Developer at the school, Grafisch Lyceum Utrecht (GLU). If you know Oudkarspel than you will know its not even close to Utrecht. So yes, i travel everyday to Utrecht and back. Its 2 hours each way, but its worth it. Because I really like my education and programming has become my passion.

Before I went to the GLU, I had never even touch a code language. I literally started from square one. In the beginning of my education I was quite struggeling and it also didn't help that I was sick in one of the first weeks. I was falling behind a bit. Then after the first few weeks school offered some extra help to the students who where just like me, struggeling a bit to keep up or didn't understand curtain things. After the first few months I maneged to turn things around. I understood everything better and it was all going better. From the moment onwards I was only in a upwards spiral. My grades got better and my motivation had never been so high. Even to this day, my moviation is incredibly high. Back in middle school, my motivation was never there. But since I go to school here, that has changed. Even tho i have to travel 4 hours every day, its so worth it. Cause this has become my passion and I am so happy with all my choices I have made. So in conclusion: you can achieve everything in life, if you work hard enough for it.
