About me



Hi, my name is Lars Pluijmakers. I am currently 19 years old, living in Oudkarspel, and my hobbies are gaming (obviously), playing chess and ice skating. My education is Game Developer at the school, Grafisch Lyceum Utrecht (GLU). If you know Oudkarspel then you will know its not even close to Utrecht. So yes, I travel everyday to Utrecht and back. It's 2 hours each way, but it's worth it. Because I really like my education and programming has become my passion.

Before I went to GLU, I had never even touched a code language. I literally started from square one. In the beginning of my education I was quite struggeling and it also didn't help that I was sick in one of the first weeks. I was falling behind a bit. Then after the first few weeks school offered some extra help to the students who where just like me, struggeling a bit to keep up or didn't understand curtain things. After the first few months I maneged to turn things around. I understood everything better. From the moment onwards I was only in a upwards spiral. My grades got better and my motivation had never been so high. Even to this day, my moviation is incredibly high. Back in middle school, my motivation was never there. But since I go to school here, that has changed. Even though I have to travel 4 hours every day, it's so worth it. Cause this has become my passion and I am so happy with all my choices I have made. So in conclusion: you can achieve everything in life, if you work hard enough for it.



My projects

On this section are my projects in short. For the more extensive version, click on the title of the game.



Roman Revenant

Our game is a top down arena surival game. You play as a Roman warrior and you have to survive against different enemies. As you progress, some enemies may become stronger and... bigger?!? Also you can buy a variety of weapons, each with different attacks and abilities. So what are you waiting for? Be THE Roman Revenant!


Tokyo Glow: Neon Night

We needed to make a race game but with glow in it, so why don't make it night themed? In this game it's your goal to drive 3 laps as fast as possible. What's your fastest time?


Dinobot: The fast bite

The final project of year one. A 3D shooter/survival game with robo-dinos. It even has a upgrade system in it to make your player stronger! Will you make it till the end?


Piggies Adventure

My first group project! This is a Shoot Them Up (SHMUP) top down game. The Goal is to survive the enemies that come from above the screen and defeat the enemy boss. Can you defeat him?
