Tokyo Glow: Neon Night

Gameplay Video

This was our most recent group project made with artists. And it was the biggest group so far, with 7 people total. Before this project, we had 2 weeks of practice on how to build the car and tweak it. So I came prepared going into this project. In this 4 week project, we make a night-themed street circuit that is based on the real streets of Tokyo. So that's why we chose the name.

What challenges did I face?

The communication with some artists wasn't too great. We both had different ideas for the game so it was hard to go on one line. For next time, I need to do more give and time. You can't be the only one deciding, sometimes you have things in your game that you don't agree with, and that's ok!

What did I make?

My main task for this project was the player. I needed to "build" the car and let it drive smoothly. It also needed to have a boost.
